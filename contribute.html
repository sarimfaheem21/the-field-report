Write for The Field Report

Open to all NYU School of Global Public Health students.

What we're looking for

400–600 word briefs summarizing a peer-reviewed paper from any country. The paper should connect to your concentration and ideally include a data visualization or tool component — Power BI, Tableau, GIS, R, Python.

Guidelines

  • Paper must be peer-reviewed and publicly accessible
  • Brief must be original writing, not a summary of the abstract
  • Include a faculty reviewer from a relevant department
  • Tag your country and topic area when submitting

What you get

Your name, program, and faculty reviewer credited publicly on the site. A real, faculty-reviewed writing sample for your portfolio and job applications.
