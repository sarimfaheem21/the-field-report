The Process

How it works

01

Select a paper

A student picks a peer-reviewed paper from any country — often one connected to their own background or interests.

02

Write the brief

400–600 words connecting the research to their concentration, using a job-relevant tool — Power BI, Tableau, GIS, and more.

03

Faculty review

A faculty member in the relevant department reviews for accuracy before the brief is published.

04

Published portfolio

The student's name, program, and faculty reviewer are credited publicly — a real writing sample for job applications.
